A Deployment is a bundle of applications that you deploy to one or more servers.

  1. To create a new deployment / application / server, select from the action menus.
  2. To assign apps or servers to your deployment, drag them on to the deployment
  3. To expand/hide details of a deployment, double-click it.
  4. To go live or undeploy, mark the checkbox and use the action menu
  5. To remove apps / servers from your deployment, mark the checkbox beside them, then use the action dropdown
